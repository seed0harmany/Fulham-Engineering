Construction Management

Oversight that protects quality, cost, and schedule.

Fulham Engineering provides construction management services designed to support successful project delivery. Our role is to ensure that design intent, quality standards, and contractual requirements are met throughout construction.

We represent our clients’ interests by coordinating stakeholders, monitoring progress, and addressing technical issues as they arise on site.

Our construction management approach emphasizes transparency, accountability, and proactive risk management.

Services Offered

  • Construction Planning and Coordination
  • Site Supervision and Quality Assurance
  • Schedule and Cost Monitoring
  • Contract Administration Support
  • Technical Issue Resolution
  • Project Closeout and Documentation
